French Toast

Description

A dish usually served for breakfast in which bread is dipped into eggs and seasonings and then fried to a golden brown. French toast is often topped with butter, preserves, or syrup. It is often used as a method to use up French bread that is a day or two old and has started to dry out.

Ingredients needed:

  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 4 slices of bread

Steps:

  1. Beat egg, vanilla and cinnamon in shallow dish with wire whisk. Stir in milk.
  2. Dip bread in egg mixture, turning to coat both sides evenly.
  3. Cook bread slices on lightly greased nonstick griddle or skillet on medium heat until browned on both sides. Serve with Easy Spiced Syrup, if desired.
  4. Easy Spiced Syrup: Add 1 teaspoon vanilla extract and 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon to 1 cup pancake syrup; stir well to mix. Serve warm, if desired.
